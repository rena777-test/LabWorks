The competitions of the final stage of the "Autumn Cup" took place. Based on its results, the winner and prize-winners of the tournament were determined.

The Mad Head competition is the fifth and final stage of the Fall Cup of the seventh season of the Battle of the Corporations. 17 teams took part in it. For two and a half hours, the participants answered the questions of the presenter on various topics, showing not only erudition, but also intuition and ingenuity.

The first team with a score of 31.5 points was the team "Nertus", the second finished last year's winners "Creator IQ" (30), and the third again were the players of "Alfa-Bank" (28.5 points).

Considering the results of performances at the previous stages (karting, darts and mini-football), the "Autumn Cup" was won by "Creator IQ". In the "last bend" the winners were able to bypass the leader of the season after the first four stages, the "Staleks", which took only seventh place at the "Mad Head" stage. The third place is taken by the employees of the "Hello" company. Consistently high results throughout all stages, especially the "silver" in mini-football competitions, allowed them to get the bronze Cup of the autumn part of the season.

In February the teams will start fighting in the new five stages of the "Spring Cup". The first is bowling.
